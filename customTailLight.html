Custom Tail Lights

We are researching techniques that will allow car owners to customize their tail lights. Our first prototype will be a custom design for a Mazda Miata. The tail lights will have different modes which will display different animations. The modes will consist of a driving mode, a game mode, and a "show" mode. Each will be explained in detail in the details section below.

DETAILS

The Modes

The custom LED tail lights will have three states or "modes" that it will be able to display. The user will be able to choose these modes from their smartphone, as well as control some of what is being displayed on the screen, such as in scrolling text or the game mode. Each mode is described below.

Driving Mode:

The driving mode will consist of all of the normal funcitonality of a normal tail light assembly. The show mode will display brake lights, tail lights, blinkers, and hazard lights.

Show Mode:

The show mode will display a number of animations that we have pre-programmed into the computer that controls the tail lights. The purpose of this mode is to give car owners the ability to show off their custom LED tail lights at car shows or to their friends wherever they are. The animations will consist of scrolling text, rainbows, twinkling, and a few others.

Game Mode:

The game mode is mostly an extendsion of the show mode. The driver will not be able to use this mode while driving, but it will be a terrific mode to show off the tail lights to admirers. The games we plan on adding are snake and pong.
